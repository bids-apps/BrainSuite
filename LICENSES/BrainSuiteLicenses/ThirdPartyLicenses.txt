Overview
-------
The software included as part of the BrainSuite package uses third party
libraries and tools, which are used under the following licenses.
This document summarizes these libraries and the licenses under which they are
used.

The BrainSuite command line programs use zlib (www.zlib.net), version 1.2.11,
Copyright (C) 1995-2017 Jean-loup Gailly and Mark Adler, under the terms of
the zlib license.

The BrainSuite package includes the programs Cerebro and CerebroHelper,
Copyright (C) 2021 The Regents of the University of California. Cerebro
and CerebroHelper are licensed under the Cerebro License, v 2.0 (see below).

Cerebro and CerebroHelper make use of the Automated Image Registration (AIR)
package, v. 5.3.0, (c) 1995-2012 Roger W. Woods, M.D.
(http://www.bmap.ucla.edu/portfolio/software/AIR/)
by permission from Roger W. Woods, M.D.

Descriptions of the third party libraries used by the BrainSuite Diffusion
Pipeline (BDP) and SVReg: Surface-Constrained Volumetric Registration (SVReg)
are provided in their respective directories and also in this document
(see below).
